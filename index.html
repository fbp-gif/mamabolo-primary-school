About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1934, Mamabolo Primary School has been a pillar of education in the Capricorn District for 92 years. We have grown from humble beginnings to become a leading educational institution with 500 learners and 19 dedicated teachers.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops learners academically, socially, and emotionally, preparing them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that nurtures every learner's potential in a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment.
